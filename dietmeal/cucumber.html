WHAT IS CUCUMBER LEMON?

This Healthy Cucumber Lemonade is the perfect naturally-sweetened refreshing drink for summer! It's made from blended cucumbers, fresh lemon juice and honey, so it's the perfect healthy refreshment for warm days and summer parties!

THE INGREDIENTS

1 16 oz. canning jar
½ of 1 medium size cucumber, cut into slices
5 cups water
½ of a lemon, sliced
¼ cup honey, plus more if desired
¼ of fresh mint leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1
Make a simple syrup by stirring the water and sugar together in a saucepan over medium heat; heat until just about to boil and the sugar has dissolved. Place in refrigerator 30 minutes, or until cool.
