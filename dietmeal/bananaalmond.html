BANANA ALMOND MILK?

it's the best source of potassium out there and would make a fabulous ingredient in a recovery drink or kids' smoothie—or, as we mentioned above, in your morning cup of coffee. Though bananas and milk are considered incompatible according to Ayurveda, there's no research to support the claim that they harm your health or digestion. Therefore, these two nutritious ingredients can be safely enjoyed in moderation as part of a healthy, well-rounded diet.

THE INGREDIENTS

2 bananas, sliced and frozen
1 cup Almond Breeze Original Almondmilk
1 Tablespoon flax seed
½ teaspoon vanilla extract
½ teaspoon cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine all ingredients in blender and pulse on high for 30 seconds to 1 minute, until mixture is completely smooth and thick. Serve immediately.
