SHARING MY LOVE FOR

Music 

 
      Pop Genre

My love for pop music has been the reason why I love singing. Pop music contains many songs that are fast-tempo and talk about believing in yourself. When you're having a bad day you listen to pop. When you're angry and need to feel better, you listen to an uplifting song, more likely a hip-hop song that's classified as pop. Pop music usually has an engaging rhythm, repetitive lyrics, and an approachable musical style that is easy to sing-along. The ability to sing along is key for a lot of people who like to listen to lively music in the car while commuting and are basically killing time.

      Kpop Genre

Kpop is very popular these days. Kpop have idols which are groups and artists formed by the various entertainment companies creating catchy Korean popular music and targeting younger audiences. The music groups are formed from a group of people who are all particularly talented in at least one of the following: singing, rapping, and dancing With kpop, there is such a huge variety of different songs out there, making it really interesting to explore and discover new songs/styles. this way, you never get bored of it and experience new things all the time. Kpop is so different to just pop music nowadays, and that is why I was naturally dragged over to the kpop world.

      Opm Genre

The majority of Philippine Music revolves around cultural influences from the West. OPM is the heart of the Filipino people including my heart. It speaks the hearts and minds of the Filipinos, and it shows how truly talented Filipinos are in the music industry. OPM is special because it was made by us and it represents us, the Philippines. I love how expressive they are when it comes to music that they create. It also inspires me how the world should surround with this kind of songs to lift you up if you are having a hard time.
