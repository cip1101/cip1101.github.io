HAVE YOU TRIED EATING SUSHI BAKE?

It’s a super popular potluck dish inspired by sushi. Essentially it’s a deconstructed California roll, layered and baked in a casserole. Everything gets warm and creamy and you scoop it up into crispy sheets of roasted seaweed and top it off with cucumber and avocado. It’s SO good. If you love California rolls, you’ll love sushi bake. It’s savory, creamy, and super satisfying.

WHERE TO ORDER SUSHI BAKE IN MANILA?

‘Sushi Bake’ trend is infiltrating all our social media pages during quarantine nonetheless. So if you don’t exactly have the skills to whip up a pan of baked sushi, here are a couple places that can do just that and more for you. Here, we've listed 3 shops where you can find the best sushi bake in Manila.


1. Taste and Tell MNL

Taste and Tell were one of the first few shops to debut the concept of Sushi Bake here in Manila, and you're guaranteed a lip-smacking experience in every bite! With the wild success of their yummy Kani Aburi Sushi Bake, Taste and Tell have added Umami Unagi, Sustēki Aburi, and Scallop x Salmon Bake into their menu.
PRICELIST:
Kani Aburi Sushi Tray (8x8 and 8x12) - starts at Php 1,200
Umami Unagi (7.5x5.5; 8x8 and 8x12) - starts at Php 1,550
Sutēki Aburi (7.5x5.5; 8x8 and 8x12) - starts at Php 1,700
Scallop x Salmon (7.5x5.5; 8x8 and 8x12) - starts at Php 1,500
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @tasteandtellmnl on Instagram


2. ABURI MAMA

With a following of over 10,000 on Instagram, you should at least be curious about the hype around Aburi Mama. Their small selection of sushi bakes have been tweaked to perfection and a quick peak at their Instagram will show you that even celebrities are devouring Aburi Mama goods. Trust us when we say that you'll want in on this hype.
PRICELIST:
Cheesy Salmon Kani Aburi (8x8; 8x12) - starts at Php 980
Bang Bang Scallop Aburi (8x8; 8x12) - starts at Php 1,450
Unagi Ikura Eggsplosion (8x8; 8x12) - starts at Php 2,200
Spicy Salmon Poké Crunch (8x8; 8x12) - starts at Php 1,650
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @aburi.mama on Instagram


3. OOMORI JAPANESE CUISINE

Oomori, a well-loved sushi spot in QC, has dipped into the Sushi Bake trend with their own take. They have four flavours, California, Salmon Truffle, Salmon Teriyaki, and Wasabi Cheese Melt; they also offer a tray that includes all four! Their sushi bake trays also come with Nori sheets.
PRICELIST:
California Sushi Bake - Php 1,450
Salmon Truffle Sushi Bake - Php 1,300
Salmon Teriyaki Sushi Bake - Php 1,300
Wasabi Cheese Melt Sushi Bake - Php 1,200
4-in-1 (all flavors) - Php 1,450
HOW TO ORDER?
Visit @oomori.ph on Instagram
