HAVE YOU TRIED EATING KOREAN CORN DOGS?

Korean hot dogs are a popular street food from Korea. Hot dogs are coated in batter and deep fried. They are then lightly coated in sugar before finished with condiments of your choice. The sweet and salty combination works surprisingly well . There are also several variations, including a mozzarella dog only filled with cheese (this one is especially popular on social media), and ones that are coated in potatoes or ramen noodles.

WHERE TO ORDER KOREAN CORN DOGS IN MANILA?

Corndogs aren’t new to us, however, the Korean version of it may be for some. Thanks to our ongoing love affair with anything Korean nowadays, this Korean crowd-favorite street food is having its spotlight moment. What’s great is that you don’t need to fly all the way to South Korea to get your hands on them. Here, we've listed 3 shops where you can find the best Korean Corn Dogs in Manila.


1. Gorae Hotdog Philippines

Gorae Hotdog is a Korean shop known for its whale-like shaped hotdogs, which is also the literal translation of its name. They are also known for hotdogs wrapped in a special dough recipe then rolled in crumbs, noodles, and other interesting ingredients.
PRICELIST:
Cheddar Jumbo Hotdog - Php 100
Gorae Hotdog - Php 50
Jumbo Hotdog - Php 75
Mozza Jumbo Hotdog- Php 75
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @goraehotdogphilippines on Instagram


2. Dogs and Dairies

Dogs and Dairies offers quite a variety of Korean corndogs you can choose from. They even recently introduced a Filipino-Korean fusion flavor called Ube Cheese Corndog. You can get a bite of their many flavors by ordering a box of 5 for only P350!
PRICELIST:
Mozza Cheese Hotdog - P70 each
Potato Cheese Hotdog - P85 each
Assorted box of five - P350
Ube Cheese Corn dog - P95 each
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @dogsanddiaries on Instagram


3. K-runch

Located at Banawe, QC, K-runch Crest offers 9 different kinds of Korean rice corndogs. From their original flavor, to squid ink and double cheese corndogs, these are surely the best snacks to munch on at anytime of the day.
PRICELIST:
Original - Php 79
Potato - Php 89
Half Sausage and Half Mozzarella - Php 99
Jumbo - Php 119
Cheddar Cheese - Php 109
Noodles - Php 79
All Mozzarella - Php 109
Squid Ink - Php 109
Double Cheese - Php 129
HOW TO ORDER?
Visit @krunchcrest on Instagram
