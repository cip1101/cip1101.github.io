HAVE YOU TRIED EATING BAKED SALMON?

This Baked Salmon With Cream Cheese is a great way to liven up your fish and really pack in the flavour! Adding the dill cream cheese to the top of the salmon keeps it so juicy and adding the wonderful flavours of dill and garlic. According to the USDA National Nutrient Database, 3 ounces (oz) or approximately 85 grams (g) of cooked Atlantic salmon contains : 175 calories. 10.5 g of fat. 0 g of carbohydrate. Salmon is perfect for the grill, especially when you compare it to white fish, because the flesh stays firm as it cooks and doesn't fall apart.

WHERE TO ORDER CREAMY BAKED SALMON IN MANILA?

Baked Salmon with cream cheese and parmesan is juicy and flaky with flavorful cream cheese and parmesan crust. Serve it as the main dish for dinner and serve it with mixed green salad, simply amazing! Here, we've listed 3 shops where you can find the best Creamy Baked Salmon in Manila.


1. Janaia's Baked Salmon

They wouldn't name the business Janaia's Baked Salmon PH if it wasn't the bestselling dish on the menu. This shop offers a huge salmon, with a creamy, cheesy, tasty topping, and then surrounded by mixed vegetables.
PRICELIST:
Chessy Baked Salmon - Php 2,149/kg
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @janaiasbakedsalmon.ph on Instagram


2. Benedicto Kitchen

Reminiscin’ this oven-baked parmesan crusted salmon, baked the way we like to eat our salmon — definitely not overcooked and not dry at all! @benedictokitchen.ph creamed corn is its perfect partner, I tell you. Get more than one order.
PRICELIST:
Baked Salmon - P1090/0.5kg
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @benedictokitchen.ph on Instagram


3. Baked Salmon PH

Of course, Baked Salmon Ph is included in this list! It is after all one of the most popular baked salmon dishes ordered during the holidays. This is made with a Norwegian pink salmon fillet with a cheesy, creamy crust, then served with peas and corns on the side.
PRICELIST:
Starts at Php 949 per 500g
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @bakedsalmonph on Instagram
