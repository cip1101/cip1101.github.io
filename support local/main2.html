HAVE YOU TRIED EATING LASAGNA?

What's not to love about the humble lasagna? This dish, which is traditionally made of alternating layers of pasta, ragu, and cheese, deserves more recognition. Lasagne are a type of wide, flat pasta, possibly one of the oldest types of pasta. Lasagne, or the singular lasagna, is an Italian dish made of stacked layers of thin flat pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù and other vegetables, cheese, and seasonings and spices such as garlic, oregano and basil.

WHERE TO ORDER LASAGNA IN MANILA?

This dish, composed of flat pasta noodles, ragout, cheese, and béchamel sauce, is not the easiest to come by in the Metro. Here, we've listed 3 shops where you can find the best Lasagna in Manila.


1. Mum's Lasagna

Looking for classic Filipino-style lasagna that's decidedly goopy and cheesy? Look no further than Mum's Lasagna's flagship Lasagna. Mum's Lasagna has their own twist of thick, creamy, and ultra-cheesy bechamel sauce.
PRICELIST:
single - Php 450
medium tray - Php 1200
party tray - Php 2300
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @mumslasagna.ph on Instagram


2. House of Lasagna

It's only fair to expect that a place named after lasagna would do the dish well—and boy, does House of Lasagna live up to expectations. Their Beef Lasagna (P295/single, P880/half tray, P1,650/whole tray) has a wonderfully savory flavor profile, with a hefty amount of ground beef.
PRICELIST:
single - Php 295
half tray - Php 880
whole tray - Php 1650
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @houseoflasagna on Instagram


3. Lasagna Fix

Made with lean beef, rich tomatoes, and their secret cream sauce, it'll be a while before you move on from their Beefy Lasagna.
PRICELIST:
sampler tray - Php 420
square tray - Php 800
family tray - Php 1200
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @lasagnafix on Instagram
