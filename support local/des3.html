HAVE YOU TRIED EATING BANANA PUDDING?

Banana pudding typically consists of layers of vanilla custard, cookies or cake, and sliced bananas topped with whipped cream or meringue. Banana pudding is a dessert generally consisting of layers of sweet vanilla flavored custard, cookies and sliced fresh bananas placed in a dish and served, topped with whipped cream or meringue. It is commonly associated with Southern American cuisine, however, it can be found around the country.

2. Cocoa & Co

The Cocoa & Co.'s vanilla pudding base is creamy and thick, and is balanced out by freshly-sliced sweet bananas, the crumbly texture of crushed cookies, and a crunch from the chunky chocolate chips, all well-mixed into one gooey spoonful. You get every ingredient (and every texture) in almost every bite, so it's also worth its already affordable price.
PRICELIST:
8 oz pint - Php 159
16 oz pint - Php 270
HOW TO ORDER?

3. M bakery

M Bakery is known for its classic banana pudding. When we tried it on our banana pudding recipe, we thought it blended perfectly."
