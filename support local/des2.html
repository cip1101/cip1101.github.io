HAVE YOU TRIED EATING KOREAN MINIMALIST CAKE?

If you've never heard of a minimalist cake, then you should know that it is a Korean food trend that has reached the Philippine shores. The reason it's called minimalist is because it is not big on food aesthetics, the type that makes you drool at the mere sight of its design. But what it lacks for arty-crafty makes for flavor!

WHERE TO ORDER KOREAN MINIMALIST CAKE IN MANILA?

Simple, clean, yet enticing is the best way to describe the minimalist cakes. Here, we've listed 3 shops where you can find the best Korean Minimalist Cakes in Manila.


1. Aegyo Cakes

Aegyo Cakes makes minimalist cakes that not only look great, but taste great, too. They are known for their 16-Layer Dark Choco Bonbon with 65% Dark Malagos Chocolate ganache and Swiss meringue buttercream frosting, and the Rainbow Velvet with a six-colored velvet cake and cream cheese filling and frosting.
PRICELIST:
4” Lunchbox Cake starts at Php 450
6” Cake starts at Php 1000
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @aegyocakes on Instagram


2. Sweet Eats by KC

For adorable looking cakes and Korean-inspired designs, you may check out the page of Sweet Eats by KC. They can add not only a sweet message on your minimalist cakes, but also capture the look of the recipient with a charming drawing.
PRICELIST:
Starts at Php 850
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @sweeteatsph on Instagram


3. Baked by Trimy

Queozn City-based Baked by Trimy makes totally aesthetic cakes customized according to your needs. For flavors, you can take your pick among Vanilla Cream, Citrus Vanilla, Caramel, Chocolate, Mocha, Strawberry and Cream, and Ube; and for styles, you can go for the Minimalist, Signature, Character, Vintage, Drip, Fondant, Painted, or Textured designs.
PRICELIST:
6” x 3” starts at Php 800
6” x 5” starts at Php 1500
8” x 3” starts at Php 1200
8” x 5” starts at Php 2300
HOW TO ORDER?
DM @bakedbytrimy on Instagram
