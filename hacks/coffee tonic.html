Coffee Tonic


The combination of bubbly tonic and the bitter espresso combine to make a drink that is complex and surprisingly refreshing.
Coffee Tonic is so simple and fast to make! It delivers so much flavour—it is appreciated especially for the balance of acidity, sweetness and bitterness. But at the same time, it also offers quite a lot of room for experimentation!

THE INGREDIENTS:

coffee (espresso)
tonic water
ice


Steps to make a perfect Coffee Tonic:










Step 1.

Fill a big glass (350 ml) all the way to the rim with ice.







Step 2.

Pour over a good tonic and let it sit whilst you’re preparing the espresso. That way the tonic has time to get ice cold. Add garnish if you like – we normally serve it with a couple of slices of lemon or lime.







Step 3.

Prepare a fruity espresso and pour the shot gently over the ice and serve it straight away.
