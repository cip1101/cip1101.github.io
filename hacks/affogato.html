Affogato

The affogato was designed to feature two of Italy's favorite things: espresso and gelato. Literally meaning "drowned," affogato first appeared in the dictionary in the early 1990s and the dessert drink has since found fans worldwide. You can make it with espresso and vanilla gelato, or switch to strongly brewed coffee and vanilla ice cream. If you like, spike it with a liqueurâ€”amaretto, hazelnut, and Irish cream are favorites.

THE INGREDIENTS:

1 scoop vanilla gelato, or ice cream
2 ounces hot espresso, or strongly brewed hot coffee
Grated chocolate, for garnish


Steps to Make It:










Step 1.

Gather the ingredients.







Step 2.

In a chilled glass or dessert dish, place a large scoop of vanilla gelato or ice cream.







Step 3.

Slowly pour the espresso or coffee over the ice cream.







Step 4.

Garnish with shaved or grated chocolate.
