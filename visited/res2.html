What is so special about Cangrejos Locos?

You probably think you’ve been there and done that when it comes to Spanish food, but with all the different regions and styles to explore, it seems we've barely scratched the surface. Cangrejos Locos over in Molito Commercial Center isn’t about to let that issue go unaddressed. The laid-back Spanish joint by Chef Robby Goco serves up irresistible starters like the Huevos Rotos, crispy fries buried under runny sunny side-up with peppery chunks of chorizo, and Croquetas Locos. The star of the show, however, is the Cangrejos Locos Paella, a combination of softshell crabs, crab fat, and stock-steeped rice that makes for a rich, perfect spoonful every time. The restaurant is open for dine-in, takeout, and delivery.
