What is so special about Romantic Baboy?

A restaurant we’ve been waiting for to open, Romantic Baboy is a KBBQ restaurant that serves up unlimited pork, beef, grade A cheese, banchan, and more. It’s for those who miss eating KBBQ. It’s not for those who are trying to be vegan or vegetarian. For pick-up or delivery orders, you may contact your preferred branch’s contact number or their Facebook page. You may also book via GrabFood or Foodpanda.
